ABOUT GMK

GMK- INTERNATIONAL PARTNERS LTD. was formed in 2005 in the United States of America (USA), then moved its offices in Kigali to get involved in the rebuild- ing of Africa and Rwanda in particular. The firm operates as a full architectural firm with headquarters in Kigali since June 2006. Our firm is operating in Rwanda, Burundi, Congo, and Mali with our partners from Kenya (Blink limited ), from Ethiopia (Sieshi Consult ), form USA (Lupton Rausch& Ohio Equity), Samoo Architects &YooIL Architects and engineers; and Mali (ASTEC, s.a.r.l)

KAMIYA Jean Marie Vianney

Mr. Kamiya's experience varies from academic research, to practice in the role of Designer, and Architect Project Manager. Before joining graduate school,Mr.Kamiya practiced in several countries of the Central African region.He joined the Lupton Rauch Architecture + Interior Design in 2005 to prepare a joint venture in Africa. He briefly worked for the Consortium OZ ARCHITECTURE and TETRA TECH during the elaboration of the preliminary design for Kigali and Rwamagana/Rwanda masterplans and finally founded GMK Architecture Ltd that has head-office is in Kigali-Rwanda and offices in Bujumbura Burundi and Bamako-Mali.

His research and professional skills in both architecture design and urban planning provide him with the critical balance between design.
